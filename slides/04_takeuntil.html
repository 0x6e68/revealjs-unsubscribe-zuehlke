What is "takeUntil"?

  • RxJs Opereator - Official Docs: takeUntil(notifier: Observable<b>) — Emits the values emitted by the source Observable until a notifier Observable emits a value.

Summary

  • takeUntil acts after given observable is fired and will unsubsribe from the observable
  • The "unsubscribe" observable must still be called in the OnDestroy block

Summary - (Dis-)advantages

  • (+) Declerative solution
  • (+) Does let the observable "complete" itself
  • (-) Call on ".next() + .complete()" in OnDestroy can easily be forgotten
  • (-) takeUntil(...) must come last on the setup or else you will get side effects
