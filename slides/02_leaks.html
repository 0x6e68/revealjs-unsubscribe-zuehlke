Leaky Observables

What are observables?

  • Observable - a "tool" from the library RxJS to make your application more "reactive"
  • "We can think of RxJS Observable as an potentially infinite async Array [...]" Tomas Trajan
  • "With Observables, we’re now dealing with zero to many values over time." Tomas Trajan
  • Strongly used in Angular

Why are they leaking?

  • Observable does not know when it is "done"
  • To get the values we have to subscribe to the obserevable
  • These subscriptions are kept alive even if the component gets destroyed!
  • They die only after a ".unsubscribe()" call
